The MYPCS Method

How the best learners
actually study.

Not our opinion. The same learning science behind Duolingo, Khan Academy, and Anki — applied to UPPCS exam prep for the first time in India.

01
📚
Micro-Notes
Learn in bites, not marathons.
Coaching dumps 200 hours of lectures and hopes something sticks. We break every chapter into bite-sized fact clusters — each one linked to the exact PYQ that tested it. You read for 5 minutes, then immediately see why it matters.
📑 5-min fact clusters 🔗 Every fact → linked PYQ 📖 Multi-source notes (4 textbooks)
Cognitive Load Theory (Sweller) + Dual Coding (Paivio) — bite-sized > marathon
02
✍️
The Practice Loop
Read. Solve. Learn from mistakes. Repeat.
Most aspirants read → highlight → re-read → re-read. That’s the least effective method known to science. Our loop: read a micro-topic, immediately solve 3–5 linked PYQs, see what you actually understood vs. what you thought you did. Mistakes link back to the exact fact you missed.
🔄 Read → Solve → Review loop ❌ Wrong answer → shows the fact ⚡ +10 XP per correct solve
Bjork & Bjork (UCLA) — retrieval after learning = 2x retention vs re-reading
03
🕑
Never Forget
The system remembers what you don’t.
You studied Harappan Civilisation last month. How much do you remember? Most aspirants cram 16 hours and forget 80% in a week. Our algorithm tracks what you’re about to forget and schedules a 3-minute review at exactly the right moment — before the memory fades.
🔔 “5 facts you’re forgetting” 📈 Memory strength per topic 🔥 Daily streak = daily habit
Spaced Repetition — Kang (2016); Duolingo’s algorithm: 12% retention boost
04
📊
Weak Spot Radar
Know exactly where you’re failing.
Coaching gives the same class to 500 students. A student at 90% in Ancient History and 30% in Polity gets zero personal guidance. Your performance data builds a live map of your strengths and gaps — chapter by chapter, topic by topic. No guessing where to study next.
🎯 Accuracy heatmap by topic 📈 Weekly progress report 💰 Set daily goals: 10/20/50 Qs
Self-Regulated Learning (SRL) — dashboards + goals = 3x strategy adoption
05
🎯
Exam Simulation
Face the real exam before exam day.
On exam day, questions from all chapters are mixed, time is ticking, and negative marking punishes guessing. Students who’ve only practiced chapter-by-chapter freeze. Our simulation mode mixes topics exactly like the real UPPSC paper — so exam day feels like practice day.
🕒 UPPSC timing + marking 🎲 Mixed chapters (interleaved) 📈 Post-test analysis report
Interleaved Practice — Rohrer (2014): nearly doubled scores on delayed tests
